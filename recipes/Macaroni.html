"Macaroni"

Macaroni is a dish made with Macaroni noodles (Hence the name) and sweet cheesy goodness. Today ill be showing you how to make this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Macaroni Noodles
  • 1/2 Stick of Velveta Cheese
  • 3/4 Cup shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. First boil your noodles in a pot until they are nice and soft
  2. Next place your Velveta cheese in the pot with your noodles and mix till evenly distubeted
  3. Lastly sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top covering the whole surface for a nice cheesy coating
  4. Enjoy!
